Hollenbeck Hall

An award-winning and architecturally-stunning building, Hollenbeck Hall serves as Wittenberg's humanities building.


Hollenbeck Hall is located on Bill Edwards Drive at the top of Myers Hollow. Hollenbeck Hall is home to the departments of Business, Communication, Economics, English, Religion, Languages, East Asian Studies, History, Philosophy, Political Science, and Women's Studies, in addition to the Office of International Education, where students interested in study abroad opportunities can find all the information they need.

Workshops are available to students in Hollenbeck Hall as well. The Writing Center and the Language Learning Center (LLC) both offer tutoring services and work spaces for students to utilize. In addition, a computer lab, accessible 24 hours a day, is located on the first floor of Hollenbeck Hall.
